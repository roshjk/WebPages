The Chinese elm tree is incredibly resilient and can thrive in both indoor and outdoor settings with adequate natural illumination. The Chinese Elm Bonsai tree is a fantastic addition of flair and grace to any coffee table and is distinguished by its leaf form and trunk structure. The Chinese Elm Bonsai tree may be pruned to continue grow new leaves in any desired form. It is the most favored bonsai tree we have. Chinese elms are semi-deciduous, and in some situations, they may lose their leaves. The Chinese Elm has received instruction.
